## 📥 Required Inputs

1. **MY PRODUCT/SERVICE:** What you are selling, including core benefits.
2. **PRICE POINT:** Price or target price range.
3. **LEAD MAGNET/OPT-IN:** How the lead entered the email list (e.g., free ebook, webinar sign-up, discount code).
4. **FUNNEL STAGE:** Select one: *Cold Lead / Warm Lead / Post-Webinar / Cart Abandonment / Post-Purchase*.

## 📋 Comprehensive Execution Arc

Generate a complete, ready-to-use **7-email nurture sequence**. For **EACH of the 7 emails**, provide:

* **Send Timing:** Specify the exact day and time relative to the opt-in (e.g., "Day 1 — Immediately after opt-in" or "Day 3 — 10:00 AM recipient timezone").
* **Subject Line Options:** Provide exactly **3 distinct options** to facilitate A/B testing:
  * **Option A:** Curiosity-driven (e.g., "The one setting you forgot to toggle...")
  * **Option B:** Benefit-driven (e.g., "Get 30% more out of your database in 10 mins")
  * **Option C:** Personalization/Urgency (e.g., "{first_name}, only 24 hours left to grab your copy")
* **Preview Text:** A punchy, high-open-rate preview snippet of **40 to 90 characters** that seamlessly continues the subject line's narrative in the recipient's inbox.
* **Full Email Body Copy:** Write the complete, ready-to-paste copy.
  * Use personalization tokens (e.g. `{first_name}`) naturally.
  * Structure copy using a **Hook (scroll stopper) $\rightarrow$ Value/Story $\rightarrow$ Single Clear Call-to-Action**.
* **Call-to-Action (CTA):** Specify the exact button/link text and the logical landing page destination.
* **Strategic Goal:** Explicitly state what psychological or behavioral transition this email accomplishes (e.g., "Establishes authority," "Defangs price objection," "Builds scarcity").

## 📅 The 7-Email Nurture Arc (Sequence Blueprint)

You must write all 7 emails following this exact narrative arc:

1. **Email 1: Welcome & Value Delivery**
   * *Goal:* Build rapport, deliver the promised opt-in/lead magnet, set exact expectations for future emails, and verify email deliverability.
2. **Email 2: Overcoming the Primary Objection**
   * *Goal:* Identify the absolute #1 reason prospects do not buy (typically price, trust, or complexity) and defang it using a compelling case study or customer success story.
3. **Email 3: Pure Actionable Value (The Teaching Email)**
   * *Goal:* Provide a highly practical, standalone tip that the user can implement in 5 minutes to achieve a "quick win." No pitching allowed.
4. **Email 4: Social Proof Spotlight**
   * *Goal:* Present a powerful testimonial or data-driven case study proving the transformation your product guarantees.
5. **Email 5: Address Objection #2 & Scarcity Introduction**
   * *Goal:* Tackle the second major objection (usually timing or implementation friction) and drop a subtle hint about upcoming deadlines or bonuses.
6. **Email 6: The Direct Pitch & Value Stack**
   * *Goal:* Present a clear, high-energy pitch stacking all benefits, immediate bonuses, and risk-reversal guarantees.
7. **Email 7: Last Chance & Scarcity Close**
   * *Goal:* Drive immediate action by highlighting the hard deadline, detailing what the prospect will miss, and concluding with a strong testimonial in the P.S.

## 🚦 Copywriting & Formatting Directives
* **Word Count:** Keep emails strictly between **150 to 300 words** to ensure high readability on mobile devices.
* **Human Tone:** Write like a helpful, intelligent human sending a personal message—not a sterile corporation. Use a warm, authoritative, and direct tone.
* **No Spam Triggers:** Strictly avoid spam-trigger vocabulary that triggers promotions tab or spam filters (e.g., do not overuse "FREE," "BUY NOW," "ACT NOW," "GUARANTEED $$$," or excessive exclamation marks).
* **Format:** Use short, single-sentence paragraphs to create visual breathing room. Bullet points should be used to list benefits clearly.
